Meet the Founder

John, Founder & CEO

Jonathan loves helping remarkable companies grow. It's a passion. He also loves building systems and practices that help the HeadFord team grow, develop their passions, and make work purpose-driven.

A graduate of the Cooper Union, Jonathan likes to apply his alma mater's founding principles - to create a union of science and of art. "I founded HeadFord in 2003 with these sort of unions in mind, whether between design and technology, marketing and sales, or between our clients and ourselves."
